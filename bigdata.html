Data Security and Privacy: Protecting sensitive data from unauthorized access and ensuring compliance with privacy regulations is crucial. Data Quality and Integration: Ensuring the accuracy, consistency, and completeness of data from various sources can be challenging. Skills Gap: There is a growing demand for professionals with the skills to collect, analyze, and interpret big data.
